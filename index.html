Since there have been no major changes to COVID-19 guidelines and protocols before the sharp decrease in COVID-19 cases in Malaysia, we can say that the vaccines have helped a lot.

The Distribution of Various COVID-19 Vaccines in Malaysia

Among all 45,627,770 doses of the vaccines administered, 50.2% of them is manafactured by Pfizer. This is not a surprise because Pfizer's COVID-19 vaccine was the first COVID-19 vaccine listed by the World Health Organisation (WHO) for emergency use. Pfizer's vaccine was also the first COVID-19 vaccine approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA). Furthermore, Pfizer's COVID-19 vaccine has 91.3% efficacy against symptomatic COVID-19.
